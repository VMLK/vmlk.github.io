Preface:

I am by no means a networking expert. It is purely a passion project exploring the potential of a fully decentralised peer-to-peer architecture and putting my own spin on it.

PTP^2 is an experimental Peer-To-Peer, Plain Text Protocol aimed at providing:

  • complete decentralisation minimising the amount of root servers
  • secure mutability
  • conclusive attribution
  • a human readable protocol
  • a reliable/fast connection
  • an Internet Protocol independent communication scheme

Implementation should also ensure a low memory overhead and minimise energy consumption.

Specification

The foundation of this protocol is the asymmetric encryption scheme as it is used to ensure the attribution and mutability of data. Data or resources are addressed using a public key. The corresponding private key is used to sign the resource so that its validity can be verified by the receiver. The signature is stored alongside its resource. A local cache and a remote cache are kept alongside a peer-list to reduce the amount of time it takes to acquire a requested resource.
